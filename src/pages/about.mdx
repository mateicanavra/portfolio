## My Philosophy

I approach product development with these core principles:

1. **Build with users, not just for them** - Continuous user research and testing are essential for creating truly useful products.

2. **Focus on value, not features** - The best products solve real problems in elegant ways, not by adding endless features.

3. **Design for transparency** - Especially with AI systems, users should understand what's happening and maintain appropriate control.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beyond the tech">
  I'm also passionate about mentoring early-career engineers and product managers, particularly those from underrepresented backgrounds. If you're looking for guidance in the tech industry, feel free to reach out.
</Callout>
